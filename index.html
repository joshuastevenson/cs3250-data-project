Speedrun.com analyisis

speedrun.com is a global leaderboard for speedruns. Data from speedrun.com is licensed under Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial 4.0 International. This site is not affiliated with speedrun.com.

This page helps explore the growth of speedrunning. By interacting with the data you can notice things like the average run length submitted on weekends is longer than overall. This could be interpreted several ways. It could be that people have time for longer runs on the weekend or less serious speedrunners do runs on the weekends while more serious runners will play all week.
